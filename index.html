FAQ

What's a planet?

An aggregation of blogs.

What about the license?

Some blogs could be published on a Creative Commons license, others no. Check each blog for license information.
We reuse the RSS streams to offer content or context excerpt, based on the blogs own configuration, under the fair use doctrine.
